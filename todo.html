How to Use the Task Manager

From this section, you can manage all your tasks easily and stay organized. Here’s how it works:

  • Add new tasks under different categories (e.g. Work, Personal, Shopping).
  • Edit your existing tasks anytime to update their details.
  • Delete tasks you no longer need with a single click.
  • Mark tasks as completed once you’ve finished them to track your progress.
  • Use filters or categories to view only the tasks you want.
💡 Tip: Organize tasks by priority or deadline for better productivity.
⚠️ Note: Deleted tasks cannot be recovered, so make sure before removing them.
Need help? Click the Help icon or contact support.

General Questions

How do I create a new task?

Simply click the “Add Task” button, fill in the required fields such as title, category, and description, then confirm. Your task will appear instantly in the selected category.

Can I edit a task after creating it?

Yes. Click the edit icon inside the task, and the text becomes editable. You can update the title or description anytime.

How do I expand or collapse a task to view details?

Click anywhere on the task card. It will open or close smoothly, showing or hiding the description.

How do categories work in this app?

Each task belongs to a specific category (e.g., Work, Personal). Tasks are automatically added to both the “All Tasks” page and their own category page.

Can I change the color or icon of a task?

Yes. When creating a task you can select a color and icon. These elements help visually identify different types of tasks.

Privacy Policy

Does the app store any personal data?

No. The app only stores task information such as titles, descriptions, and categories—never personal user data.

Where are my tasks saved?

Your tasks are stored locally in your browser’s storage. This means no external server receives or tracks your information.

Do you track user behavior or activity?

Absolutely not. We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking tools. Your activity stays on your device.

Are my tasks synced across devices?

Not by default. Since tasks are stored locally, they are only available on the same device and browser unless you manually export/import your data.

Can anyone else access my tasks?

Only if they have access to your device. The app itself does not share or upload any data.

Support

Why can’t I edit a task?

Make sure you clicked the edit icon. Some tasks are locked in "completed" mode and must be returned to active status before editing.

Why doesn’t the task open when I click it?

This usually happens if a child element blocks the click event. Ensure the SVG icons are clicked correctly or try clicking outside them.

I completed a task, but it didn’t move to the completed page. What should I do?

Check if the “complete” icon is functioning correctly. The app requires your browser to allow JavaScript actions like removing and appending elements.

Why are my tasks missing after I refresh the page?

If the app isn’t using persistent storage yet, tasks may not be saved. Make sure the feature is enabled in your version or update the app.

How can I report a bug or request a new feature?

You can contact the support team through the “Help & Support” section in the app menu or send feedback directly from the settings page.
